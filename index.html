Our Heritage Journey

From humble beginnings to India's premier catering service - discover our remarkable journey of culinary excellence

2008

Foundation & First Steps

Started as a small family business in Dungarpur, Rajasthan with just 3 family members, a passion for authentic Indian cuisine, and grandmother's traditional recipes passed down through generations. Our first kitchen was a modest 200 sq ft space.

3 Team Members 15 Traditional Recipes 1 Small Kitchen
2010

Growing Recognition

Expanded our team to 12 members and started catering for local weddings and festivals. Introduced our signature Royal Thali and gained recognition in Dungarpur's catering community for authentic flavors and exceptional service quality.

12 Team Members 50+ Events Local Recognition
2012

First Major Corporate Contract

Secured our breakthrough corporate catering contract with TechMahindra, serving 500+ employees daily. This milestone marked our entry into the corporate catering sector and established our reputation for large-scale operations.

25 Team Members Corporate Entry 500+ Daily Meals
2014

Multi-City Expansion

Opened our second kitchen in Pune and third in Delhi, expanding our reach across major Indian cities. Introduced live cooking stations and interactive food counters, revolutionizing the catering experience with entertainment value.

3 Cities Live Cooking 50+ Team Members
2016

ISO Certification & Quality Excellence

Achieved ISO 22000 certification for food safety management and FSSAI licensing, ensuring highest quality standards. Implemented state-of-the-art kitchen technology and temperature-controlled delivery systems for food safety.

ISO 22000 Temperature Control Quality Labs
2018

Industry Awards & Recognition

Won 'Best Catering Service' at the Indian Hospitality Awards and 'Excellence in Food Quality' at National Food Safety Awards. Crossed the milestone of 5,000 successful events and established our culinary training academy.

2 Major Awards 5,000+ Events Training Academy
2020

Digital Transformation & Innovation

Launched our comprehensive online ordering platform and contactless delivery services during the pandemic. Introduced virtual event catering, home delivery packages, and AI-powered menu customization for enhanced customer experience.

Online Platform Contactless Delivery AI Menu System
2022

National Expansion & Sustainability

Expanded operations to 15 major Indian cities with 25 master chefs and 200+ team members. Launched our sustainability initiative with eco-friendly packaging, zero-waste kitchens, and organic ingredient sourcing from local farmers.

15 Cities Eco-Friendly 200+ Team
2024

Industry Leadership & Future Vision

Recognized as India's premier catering service with 10,000+ successful events, 4.9/5 customer rating, and nationwide presence. Launching franchise opportunities and international expansion plans while maintaining our commitment to authentic Indian cuisine.

4.9/5 Rating 10,000+ Events Global Vision
